2025

Best Of

Another year, another 15 items chosen.

Top 5

Games

01

Clair Obscur: Expedition 33

April 24, 2025

Drawing clear inspiration from its predecessors like FINAL FANTASY X and Xenoblade Chronicles 3, Clair Obscur manages to stand among them and deliver a powerful story.

02

Trails in the Sky 1st Chapter

September 19, 2025

Perfect in execution, pacing and scope, this is an RPG that crafts the bedrock for all future games in the Trails series!

03

Split Fiction

March 6, 2025

YMMV but probably the best story-based couch co-op game ever made. The antagonist was way funnier than he had any right to be.

04

Donkey Kong Bananza

July 17, 2025

The first Donkey Kong game I've liked! An exceptional 3D platformer.

05

Death Stranding 2: On the Beach

June 26, 2025

More Walking, but this time with even more luxurious, cinematic cutscenes.

Top 5

Movies

01

Chainsaw Man - The Movie: Reze Arc

October 29, 2025

So good I saw this twice in theaters! See also: peak.

02

Kaguya-sama: Love Is War -Stairway to Adulthood-

December 31, 2025

Arguably not a movie? But it premiered in one hour-long contiguous chunk so it's going here. The fearful anxiety that stems from a first relationship - or perhaps any new one? - can be tough to communicate over video, but this did a great job.

03

One Battle After Another

September 26, 2025

I don't usually like action thrillers, but this movie gave its characters ample time to shine and accentuated the fight scenes.

04

Rental Family

November 21, 2025

Explores nuances of human connection across language and culture that aren't usually touched on in movies - tells a few interesting stories that intersect, and has great pacing.

05

F1

June 27, 2025

Silly racing movie that was more fun to watch than it should have been.

Top 5

TV Shows

01

Severance S2

January 17, 2025 - March 21, 2025

A powerful payoff to Season 1, the writers for this continue to interweave new mysteries into the narrative like clockwork while setting up for the eventual end.

02

Pluribus

November 7, 2025 - December 24, 2025

On the first episode I was prepared to write it off as perhaps derivative of Invasion of the Body Snatchers - glad I was wrong! A great, suspenseful sci-fi mystery.

03

Ave Mujica: The Die is Cast

January 2, 2025 - March 27, 2025

Melodrama + band + existentialism = Ave Mujica made me cry the most of any show this year.

04

The Rehearsal S2

April 20, 2025 - May 25, 2025

Maybe a bit more on-rails than Season 1? Everything Nathan Fielder makes continues to be prestige television.

05

Re:Zero - Starting Life in Another World 3

October 2, 2024 - March 26, 2025

If you liked episodes 1-50 of Re:Zero, you might just like episodes 51-66 of Re:Zero.
