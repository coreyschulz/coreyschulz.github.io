About Eric Schulz

Marketing consultant, corporate trainer, university professor, and author

A Unique Blend of Experience

ERIC SCHULZ is a marketing consultant & corporate trainer. He is part of the marketing faculty at the Jon M Huntsman School of Business at Utah State University. He previously taught at Brigham Young University and The University of Utah.

Eric is a thought-leader within the marketing industry and academia. His real-world experience combined with cutting-edge marketing tools, creativity & insights continually breaks new ground in industry best practices.

Career Highlights

Fortune 500 Experience

Eric honed his marketing skills over two decades working at some of the world's top consumer product companies – Procter & Gamble, The Walt Disney Company, and The Coca-Cola Company.

He also worked in sports and entertainment marketing with the XFL Football League, Utah Jazz basketball team, minor league baseball, Ringling Bros. & Barnum & Bailey Circus, Disney on Ice, and more.

Consulting Impact

A small sample of consulting clients Eric has helped include Nestlé, Kraft, Cardinal Health, Cox Cable, Procter & Gamble, eBay, and PayPal.

He has consulted for The Wasatch Group, Campion Scientific, Utah Governor's Committee for the Employment of People with Disabilities, and many others.

Academic Leadership

Eric brings his industry experience to the classroom as a member of the marketing faculty at the Jon M Huntsman School of Business at Utah State University.

His teaching philosophy combines academic rigor with practical, real-world applications – the same approach he brings to corporate training and consulting.
