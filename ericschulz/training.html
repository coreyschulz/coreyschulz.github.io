Corporate Training Programs

Transform Your Marketing Team

Eric offers Half-Day, 1-Day and Two-Day Corporate Training Workshops which can be customized to your specific needs.

Drawing on 25+ years of experience at companies like Procter & Gamble, Coca-Cola, and Disney, Eric delivers practical, immediately applicable training that combines Fortune 500 best practices with real-world case studies.

Whether you need to align your leadership team, upskill your marketing department, or bring fresh perspective to your strategic planning, these workshops deliver measurable impact.
